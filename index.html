Who We Are

Rooted in faith,
moving in love

Agape was born from the conviction that unconditional love — the agape described in 1 Corinthians — is not merely a spiritual concept but a practical call to action. We began with a small group of volunteers and a single community in need.

Today we walk alongside children, families, and entire communities — providing education, mentorship, welfare support, and development programmes that create lasting change rooted in dignity and faith.

  • Unconditional Love — Every person matters, regardless of circumstance.
  • Holistic Development — Addressing spirit, mind, and body together.
  • Sustainable Impact — Building capacity so communities thrive long-term.
"We do not serve because the world will change in a day — we serve because every child deserves to know they are loved today."
— Agape Mission
What We Do

Programs changing lives

Our work is organised around three pillars that reflect our belief in the whole-person development of every individual we serve.

01

Child Welfare & Care

We provide orphaned and vulnerable children with safe shelter, nutritious meals, healthcare access, and a nurturing environment grounded in faith and love.

Children · Protection
02

Education for All

Scholarships, school supplies, tutoring and mentorship programmes ensuring every child — regardless of background — can access quality education.

Education · Scholarships
03

Community Development

Vocational training, economic empowerment workshops, and infrastructure support that equip communities to build dignified, self-sustaining futures together.
